About me

Front-end developer from Johannesburg

What I do: I offer a comprehensive range of web development services designed to meet the unique needs of my clients. Whether you're a small business looking to create your first website or an established company seeking to enhance your online presence, I can help.

Web Development: My web development services include: Custom Website Design: Tailored designs that reflect your brand identity and engage your target audience. Responsive Design: Websites that look and perform perfectly on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. Cross-Browser Testing: Making sure your website functions correctly across all major browsers. Responsive Testing: Verifying that your site adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes and devices. Development: Bringing the design to life with clean, efficient code.
